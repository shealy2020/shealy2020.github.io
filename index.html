TW x AI

Navigating the intersections of technical writing and AI

The metaphors we use to understand our own interactions with AI change as quickly as we form them: from AI as digital intern to co-collaborator to document creator.

In practical terms, I want to cut through the hype to better understand the intersections of technical writing and AI as they exist today and gain a clearer idea of what the field of technical communication can expect in the years ahead.

My name is Sean Healy. I've worked in the software industry for over 15 years, mostly within documentation teams, where I wrangled DITA as a technical writer, content strategist, and developer.

These posts chronicle my exploration into the Wild West of AI. While they are not tutorials, if you can take something from my travels, you are welcome to it. I'll push prototypes and other artifacts to GitHub and continue to update the Resources page, so you'll have jumping-off points for your own journey.

Feel free to contact me if you'd like more details about any of these posts.
